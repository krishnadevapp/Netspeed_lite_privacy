Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Welcome to Netspeed Lite. We respect your privacy and confirm that we do not collect any personal data through this application.

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII), browsing history, or IP addresses.
  • Network Usage Stats: The Usage Access permission is used only to read historical network traffic logs. All processing occurs entirely on your device.
  • Local Storage: App preferences and manually tracked counters are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences and are removed when the app is uninstalled.

2. Permissions

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to keep the speed meter active in the status bar.
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS: Required to display historical data usage (Today, Last 7 Days, etc.).
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to display the live network speed indicator.

3. Disclaimer

Netspeed Lite is provided “AS IS”. Network usage statistics are estimates based on device-level logs and interpolation methods. These values may differ from your carrier’s official records. We do not guarantee accuracy and are not responsible for any data charges incurred.
